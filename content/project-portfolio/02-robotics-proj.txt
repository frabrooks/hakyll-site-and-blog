A <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constructivism_(philosophy_of_mathematics)">constructive</a> formalisation of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hoare_logic">Hoare Logic</a> in the interactive theorem prover <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agda_(programming_language)">Agda</a>. The final result is a constructive formalisation of Hoare logic and an Agda library that is fit for the purpose of proving correct simple imperative-style programs using the formalised Hoare logic.
